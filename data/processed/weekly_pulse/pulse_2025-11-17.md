# Weekly Product Pulse: Key User Feedback (Nov 17-23, 2025)
_Week: 2025-11-17 - 2025-11-23_

This week's user feedback highlights critical challenges primarily around app stability, high charges, and payment flexibility. Users are reporting significant technical glitches impacting trading, concerns about increasing brokerage fees compared to competitors, and a desire for more agile withdrawal options, including faster access to funds.

## Themes
- **Glitches & Bugs** - Users are frequently encountering critical technical issues, including app freezing, data inaccuracies, and order execution problems, severely impacting their trading experience.
- **Fees & Charges** - Widespread dissatisfaction persists regarding the platform's brokerage fees, perceived as high and less competitive, prompting users to consider alternative platforms.
- **Payments** - Users express a strong desire for enhanced withdrawal flexibility and faster access to funds, with some reporting negative experiences and delays in redeeming liquid funds.
