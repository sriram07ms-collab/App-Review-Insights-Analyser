# Weekly Product Pulse: Nov 17-23, 2025
_Week: 2025-11-17 - 2025-11-23_

This week's pulse highlights critical user dissatisfaction stemming from significant challenges in customer support and payment processing. Users frequently report unresponsiveness and ineffective support, alongside issues like delayed refunds, unauthorized deductions, and withdrawal problems, significantly impacting the overall product experience and trust.

## Themes
- **Customer Support** - Users report highly negative experiences with customer support, citing unresponsiveness, long wait times, and ineffective resolutions for critical account issues.
- **Payments** - Users frequently encounter severe negative issues related to payments, including unauthorized deductions, prolonged refund delays, and withdrawal problems impacting access to funds.

## Quotes
- "Customer Support: the custmer support team very bad no one response when u got a problem"
- "Customer Support: customer support is pathetic and useless don't use this app"
- "Payments: Paisa kat liya customer help nahin karta hai... refund nahin mila 3 Mahina ho chuka hai"

## Actions
- Conduct an urgent audit of customer support channels and agent training to improve response times and resolution efficacy for user queries.
- Prioritize investigation and resolution of all reported unauthorized deductions and long-pending refunds, with proactive user communication.
- Review and optimize payment processing workflows to ensure timely liquid fund redemptions and reliable withdrawal functionality within stated timeframes.
