# Weekly Product Pulse: November 17-23, 2025
_Week: 2025-11-17 - 2025-11-23_

This week's pulse highlights user frustration with persistent technical glitches, notably impacting app stability and data accuracy. Payment-related issues, including delayed refunds and withdrawal problems, remain a significant concern. Additionally, users are actively requesting enhanced trading features like Stop Loss/Target orders and improved charting tools to elevate their experience.

## Themes
- **Glitches & Bugs** - Users are highly frustrated by persistent technical issues, impacting app stability and data accuracy across various functionalities.
- **Payments** - Significant dissatisfaction stems from unresolved payment issues, including delayed refunds, withdrawal problems, and perceived unhelpful customer support.
- **Trading Features** - There is a strong demand for advanced trading functionalities and improvements to existing order types and charting tools.

## Quotes
- "Glitches & Bugs: Doesn't exit when trying to exit, hangs."
- "Payments: Paisa kat liya customer help nahin karta hai Mera 3 mutual fund rejected ho gaya hai abhi tak refund nahin mila 3 Mahina ho chuka hai"
- "Trading Features: Please add SL & TARGET set in chart for equity intraday trading in mobile app..."

## Actions
- Prioritize investigation and resolution of critical technical glitches, especially those affecting data consistency and application freezes.
- Review and streamline the payment and refund process to ensure timely resolution of financial discrepancies and improve communication.
- Evaluate and plan for enhancements to core trading features, including SL/Target orders and more detailed charting tools.
