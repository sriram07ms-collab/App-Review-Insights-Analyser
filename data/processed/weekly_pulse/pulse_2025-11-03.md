# Weekly Product Pulse: Nov 3-9, 2025
_Week: 2025-11-03 - 2025-11-09_

This week's pulse highlights significant user friction points. We observe critical performance issues with data glitches and a bug impacting video access. Additionally, customers report prolonged delays in F&O segment activation and express frustration over unexpected charges for routine profile updates. Addressing these areas is crucial for improving user experience.

## Themes
- **Slow, Glitches** - Users are experiencing critical intermittent data freezing and a new bug preventing IPO-related video access, leading to significant frustration.
- **Customer Support** - Customers face persistent delays and technical hurdles in activating key account segments, indicating a bottleneck in resolution processes.
- **Payments/Statements** - Users are unexpectedly charged for basic profile updates like email changes, raising concerns about transparency and fairness in fees.

## Quotes
- "price and chart stuck some time (Glitches)"
- "their technical team/system is unable to activate the F&O segment since last 7 days. (Customer Support)"
- "charged extra money for updating your email id. (Payments/Statements)"

## Actions
- Prioritize investigation and resolution of the intermittent data freezing and IPO video access bug immediately.
- Evaluate and streamline the F&O segment activation process to reduce delays and improve customer support efficiency.
- Review the fee structure for basic profile updates and enhance transparency regarding all charges for account management.
