# Weekly Product Pulse: Nov 24 - Nov 30, 2025
_Week: 2025-11-24 - 2025-11-30_

This week's pulse highlights recurring technical issues affecting core functionality, notably option chain and live value displays. Concurrently, users continue to express interest in enhancing trading capabilities with specific analytical tools, commodity alerts, and improved multi-account management features.

## Themes
- **Glitches & Bugs** - User feedback indicates significant frustration with persistent technical errors, particularly impacting critical features like option chains and live value accuracy, which are reported as intermittent or incorrect.
- **Trading Features** - Users actively request new analytical tools, such as stochastics indicators, a commodity alert feature, and improved support for displaying specific Nifty indexes and managing multiple family accounts.

## Quotes
- "Glitches & Bugs: Too much glitch, option chain not open properly sometime working sometimes not working."
- "Glitches & Bugs: something went wrong, live values cannot be shown, you are doing this frequently"
- "Trading Features: Can we have an option to view family accounts, I keep logging out from one account to check the other?"

## Actions
- Prioritize investigation and resolution of reported technical errors impacting option chain functionality and real-time data display accuracy.
- Evaluate the feasibility and roadmap for integrating requested analytical tools like stochastics indicators and a commodity alert feature into the platform.
- Explore solutions for enhanced multi-account management within the application to improve the user experience for those managing family portfolios.
