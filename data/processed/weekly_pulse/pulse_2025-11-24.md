# Product Pulse: Key Insights (Nov 24 - Nov 30, 2025)
_Week: 2025-11-24 - 2025-11-30_

This weekly pulse highlights user feedback from November 24-30, 2025. Technical glitches remain a primary concern, frequently affecting core features and data accuracy. Conversely, the application receives positive remarks for its suitability for new investors and a user-friendly interface, positioning it well for market entry.

## Themes
- **Glitches & Bugs** - Users consistently express negative sentiment regarding frequent technical errors, particularly issues with option chains, trending lines, and real-time data accuracy, which significantly impact usability.
- **App Suitability** - Users convey positive sentiment towards the application's ease of use and simpler interface, identifying it as an ideal platform for new users beginning their investment portfolios.

## Quotes
- "Too much glitch, option chain not open properly sometime working sometime not working."
- "something went wrong, live values cannot be shown, you are doing this frequently"
- "The right app to get started with your portfolio!"

## Actions
- Initiate an urgent sprint to address frequently reported technical glitches, specifically focusing on option chain functionality and trending line accuracy.
- Implement improved real-time data validation and error handling to eliminate discrepancies in share price and profit/loss calculations.
- Develop targeted onboarding campaigns highlighting the app's simplicity and suitability for new investors, leveraging this positive perception.
